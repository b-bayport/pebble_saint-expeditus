Saint Expeditus. You who are a Holy warrior. You who are the Saint of the afflicted. You who are the Saint of the desperate, you who are the Saint of urgent causes, Protect me, Help me, Give me Strength, Courage, and Serenity. Hear my plea.

My Saint Expeditus, help me prevail through these difficult hours, protect me from all those who want to harm me, respond to my plea with urgency. Bring me back to the state of peace and tranquillity, my Saint Expeditus. I will be grateful to you for the rest of my life and I will speak your name to all those who have faith. Amen.
